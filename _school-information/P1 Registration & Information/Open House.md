<h3>Open House 2026</h3>
<p>Beacon Primary School is having our Open House on Saturday, 30 May 2026,
from 9 am to 12 pm. Come and embark on an immersive Beacon Experience and
learn more about the school’s curriculum, distinctive programmes and our
students’ CCA experiences!</p>
<p>A dialogue session with our principal will be from 9.30 am to 10.15 am.
Registration is needed, and entry will be from 9.00 am only for parents
who are attending the session.</p>
<p>Booths showcasing exhibits of learning and student development experiences
will be opened from 10.00 am onwards. School tours will be conducted from
10.15 am – 11.15 am at 15-min intervals.</p>
<p>Click <a href="https://go.gov.sg/beaconopenhouse2026" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/beaconopenhouse2026</a> to
register for the Open House.</p>
