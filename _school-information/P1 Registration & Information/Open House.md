<h3>Open House 2025</h3>
<p>Beacon Primary School is having our Open House on Saturday, 31 May 2025
from 10am to 12pm. Come and embark on an immersive Beacon Experience and
learn more about the school’s curriculum, distinctive programmes and our
students’ CCA experiences!</p>
<p>There will be a dialogue session with Beacon Pri's Principal from 9 to
10am. Entry from 8.40am only for parents who are attending the session.
Open House stations will be opened from 10am onwards.</p>
<p><a href="https://go.gov.sg/beaconopenhouse2025" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Click here to register for the Open House.</a>
</p>
